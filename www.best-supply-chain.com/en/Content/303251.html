Shenzhen Best International Supply Chain Co., Ltd. was founded in 2018, focusing on cross-border logistics services for many years, the business covers: the United States sea special line, the United States air special line, the European air special line, the European sea special line, the Chinese and European railroad special line, the British sea special line, the company has always implemented the “honesty, service first, win-win” service principle. The company always carries out the service tenet of “integrity-based, service-oriented, win-win cooperation”, and enhances the market resilience and competitiveness together with customers. Provide logistics consulting and program design as a body of comprehensive European and American international logistics enterprises, enhance the value of services, logistics road with all new and old customers wish to go forward together with the new glory!
